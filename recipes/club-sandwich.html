Cuban Club Sandwich

An easy, quick, and delicious sandwich to eat anytime

A hearty sandwich filled to the brim with all the classic ingredients that make a club one of the best choices for any event or outing you may have

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bacon
  • 3 Slices of bread, toasted
  • 3 Tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 Leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of cooked turkey breast
  • 2 Slice tomato

Instructions

  1. Place bacon in a heavy skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain on paper towel.
  2. Spread each slice of bread with mayonnaise. On one slice of toast, place the turkey and lettuce.
  3. Cover with a slice of toast, then the bacon and tomato.
  4. Top with the last slice of toast.
  5. Enjoy!
