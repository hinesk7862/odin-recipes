Lobster Rolls

A simple lobster roll for cookouts and get togethers.

This is a wonderful summer (or winter) treat and makes a fancy luncheon to impress your friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 rolls (hot dog buns)
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 1 pound of cubed lobster meat
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 medium green onions
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Lightly butter the insides of the buns and line with lettuce leaves. Set aside
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together mayonnaise, lime, salt and pepper until well blended. Mix in the green onion, then mix in the lobster.
  3. Stuff the lobster filling into the buns and sprinkle parsley, basil or tarragon lightly over the filling.
  4. Enjoy!
