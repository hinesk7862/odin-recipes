Italian Sandiwch

A classic italian sandwich on a sub-roll with heaps of vegetables and cold cuts

The italian-style sandwich has been around forever, and is incredibly popular as a quick meal to grab on a lunch break or on the way home from a long shift at work. The sandwich has become a staple of corporate meetings and famliy events alike, so lets put together a fantastic sandwich that reinvents the class we all know!

Ingredients

  • Sub roll
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell Peppers
  • Black Olives
  • Lettuce
  • Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Cold cuts of your choice!

Instructions

  1. Dice 1 tomatoes, 1 bell peppers, and 1/4 cup of olives finely and toss in a bowl with 2 tablespoons of both oil and vinegar.
  2. If necessary, cut cold cuts to fit inside of the sub roll.
  3. Split the sub roll and lightly drizzle oil and vinegar onto one side
  4. Place a layer of lettuce onto the roll.
  5. Use a spoon to put the diced vegetables onto the lettuce.
  6. Lastly, put your choice of cold cuts inside the roll.
  7. Enjoy!
